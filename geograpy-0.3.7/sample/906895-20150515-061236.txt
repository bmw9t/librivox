rasorial

1. Of or pertaining to the Egyptian Sun God Ra.
2. Pertaining to barbers and surgeons in medieval times.
3. Of, or having the quality of being sharp, jagged, or razor-like.
4. Scratching the ground for food like a bird.
5. The angle a plane's wings make with the horizontal. 
6. Pertaining to an Ethiopian nobleman.
7. Pertaining to or like the proverbial rascal, as in Every rascal is not a thief, but every thief is a rascal.
